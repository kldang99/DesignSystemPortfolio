Accessibility Considerations

To ensure the website is accessible, the image of each card will include an alt text description. This is important for users who rely on screen readers as it will help them understand the visual content. Alt text descriptions will be crafted to convey specific information, instead of generic placeholders like "image." The button will have concise yet descriptive labels to communicate their purpose. These buttons will also have sufficient size and spacing, ensuring usability for those with motor impairments. The color contrast between the text and background will also meet accessibility standards (WCAG).
